Market Share

Measure your activity performance in e-commerce.

We aggregate different data sources, apply data science and obtain reliable information about:

  • Audience and traffic by retailer
  • Conversion rates by retailer
  • Market share of each competitor
  • Sales by category and product types
  • Analysis of pure players and generalist players

Price Tracking

Monitor prices across your categories and retailers online. We automate the process of capturing and analysing competitor prices, its changes and discover unauthorized vendors.

Product Categorization
